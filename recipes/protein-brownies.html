Protein Brownies

Description:

Low calorie and high protein brownies, great for a snack when feeling hungry! For a 20x20 cm pan!

Ingredients:

  • 63g cocoa powder
  • 36g protein powder (chocolate)
  • 135g erythritol
  • 0,5 teaspoon baking powder and a pinch of salt
  • 360ml diet coke

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix all dry ingredients
  3. Slowly add diet coke while stirring
  4. Bake 30 minutes, check at 20, shold be fudgy but not liquid
