Protein Apple-Pie

Description:

Apple pie with great taste and extra protein, great as breakfast or dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 600g bread
  • 2l egg whites
  • 21g cinnamon
  • 15g vanilla extract
  • 15 packets sweetener
  • 1,5Kg apples

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 200Â°C
  2. Chop apples in small pieces
  3. Rip bread in pieces and place in a casserole with the other ingredients
  4. Place the casserole dish uncovered in the oven, cook for 40-50 minutes
