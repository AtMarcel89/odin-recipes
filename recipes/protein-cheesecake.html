Protein Cheesecake

Description:

High protein cheesecake, great if you want to eat something sweet and filling without missing on the proteins!

Ingredients:

  • 350g Fat free cream cheese
  • 350g Fat free greek yogurt
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 60g Protein powder
  • 100g Erythritol
    • '
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 160°C
  2. Mix all ingredients together för 2-3 minutes with a mixer
  3. Use a 15x5cm cake pan, apply parchment paper to the cake pan
  4. Pour the mix in the pan
  5. Cook for 30-35 minutes, the 50-15 minutes at 100°C
  6. Do toothpick test to see if the inside is done, then wait for 2-3 hours before eating
